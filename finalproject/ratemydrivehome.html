What is Rate My Drive

Rate my drive is an app where users can submit reviews for the route, instead of the destinations. These reviews can include traffic, road and weather conditions. This is really useful for tourists and people driving to unknown locations, where they may not know the road.

McLaughlin’s project was featured at the virtual ‘Canada-Wide Science Fair’ 2021.

CJLS news

A local student has received national recognition for his technology project. Charlie McLaughlin is in Grade 10 at Yarmouth Consolidated Memorial High School, and is one of 61 students across Canada to receive the Ted Rogers Innovation Award. McLaughlin impressed at his local Science, Technology, Engineering and Math fair with his application, ‘Rate My Drive.’
